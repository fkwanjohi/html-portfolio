The Best Movies According to Freddie

My top 3 movies of all time

Good Will Hunting

I loved math growing up, and this movie is about a math genius who grew up as a foster kid in south Boston. It's a beautiful, coming of age story.
Matt Damon and Robin Williams have a great dynamic.

Iron Man

This movie made me fall in love with Marvel and more specifically the MCU.
Robert Downey is the perfect Tony Stark.

Avengers: Endgame

In my opinion, this movie was the climax of the best of the MCU. Absolute masterpiece with an incredible cast, well befitting their roles.
